About Me

My name is James Woodcock. I'm an aspiring Web Developer hoping to expand my skills. I mainly have practice in HTML and CSS, but I'm learning JavaScript and later down the line I'll be learning Python. In my free time I'll normally play various video games or go to my local Dungeons & Dragons group.

Background

I was born in Alaska but raised in New York. I've moved around through 4 different school districts but finally settled down in Massena, NY. I have 2 siblings of which I am the oldest.

Growing up I played a lot of shooters, survival, and rhythm games. I knew gaming was my passion ever since I played Call Of Duty for the first time back in 2012. Ever since then, I've tried many other games, some good, some bad.
